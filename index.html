Pollution

Description About Pollution

Pollution is when harmful materials which are called pollutants are released into the environment, Pollutants can be created by nature itself by volcanic ash, These harmful gases destroy the environment and animals. there are many types of pollution - Air pollution, water pollution, soil pollution, light pollution, etc.




Causes and effects of pollution -

Causes of pollution -

There are various causes for pollution, EX - the burning of fossil fuels, agricultural activities, waste in landfills, mining operations, transportation, factories, power plants, wildfires. More than 2.4 million pounds of carbon dioxide are released into the atmosphere EVERY SECOND.



Effects of pollution on the human body, and environment

There are many effects that harm the human body and environment. Exposure to high pollution levels can cause various diseases. It increases the risk of respiratory diseases, heart attacks/strokes, and lung cancer/infections. Both short and long-term exposure to air pollutants has been associated with health impacts. Pollution leads to climate change, High pollution levels can cause acid rain, deterioration of fields, and extinction of animals. Pollution makes muddy landscapes, poison soils, and waterways, It kills plants and animals. Humanes also regularly die due to pollution
